BUSINESS EXCELLENCE for local Content

... Benchmarking your Business against the best in the World Striving for excellence in business means more business, more capital and more opportunities to do more business. That is why we have developed the Business Excellence Matrix and Operational Standardization for local content businesses operating in energy, oil and gas, manufacturing, information & communications technology, who seek to attract international investment, international business partnerships and international business opportunities. Our evaluation and business excellence scoring process has been developed working closely with the International Oil Companies and the Nigeria Content Development and Monitoring Board, using international bench marks and contract tender specifications. We currently support the Nigeria Content segment of the Oil and Gas Industry with its Business Excellence formatting, evaluation, accreditation and training. Our Business Excellence Matrix is divided into two strategic intervention areas, the first being an annual retreat in which executives can be kept abreast of what they need to do in order to attain an excellent status globally, and the second being a business operational evaluation which provides local firms with the guides to build globally excellent businesses.
