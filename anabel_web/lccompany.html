LOCAL COMPANIES

Our Local Content Strategy promotes the inclusion of all Local Content providers in production, operations, projects, vessels, drilling, engineering services, and well head engineering in Africa’s energy industry and in particular the oil and gas industry. In recognition of our efforts, we are currently working with the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board as operator for the NCDMB’s Project Triple E designed to support the enhanced capacity and the development of special products, special skills and special groups within the oil and gas industry including but not limited to entrepreneurs, micro, small and medium scale enterprises as well as youths and women. The NCDMB’s Project Triple E is also targeted at supporting the financial structure of Nigeria Content companies; supporting the development of communities as well as the empowerment and training of youths.
The Anabel Group is working for the strategic positioning of locally manufactured goods and Nigerian oil and gas service companies that have been certified and are qualified Nigeria Content product or service providers. We are creating opportunities for partnership and encouraging the development of indigenous SMEs with the tenacity to take the Nigerian oil and gas industry to the next level. Our work with the Nigeria Content Development and Monitoring Board has exposed us to a very unique range of opportunities for Local Content providers which have not been exploited, largely because most Nigerian firms operating in the oil and gas industry have not yet seen the need to develop a comprehensive Local Content Strategy.

SUPPORT FOR LOCAL CONTENT MANUFACTURERS & SERVICES

At the Anabel Group, we are making a shift in business practices in the oil and gas sector, allowing you more access to international oil and gas companies and original equipment manufacturers’ partnerships. Through our network with international energy companies we are able to bridge the gap between you and them ensuring that your services become a part of the value chain of Africa’s oil and gas production processes.

WORLD CLASS OPERATIONS AND STRATEGIC SKILLS

Our work with oil and gas service providers on behalf of Regulators such as the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board provides us with a unique insight into the support that is available to you as well as the knowledge you will need to succeed over the long term as you build your business empire. We can extend that knowledge and expertise to supporting you directly to gain the skills to win some of the most sophisticated and lucrative tenders in the different energy markets of Africa and especially the Nigerian oil and gas industry.


FOSTERING JOINT VENTURE PARTNERSHIPS

With our network, we will match make you with international oil and gas companies and international original equipment manufacturers looking to meet their local content quota. In fulfilling our mandate to support the inclusion of local content, we will walk you through the process, develop your capacity, train your staff, and go the extra mile to ensure that you and your personnel have the sophistication and the skills required to consummate relationships with the international oil & gas companies.

PROJECT SUPPORT INSTRUMENT (FUNDING SUPPORT)

In addition to Local Content business strategy, we also specialize in financial strategy, supporting you to raise international funding needed to support your local projects, which may include; IOC contracts, drilling operations, yard operations, production or fabrication facilities, vessel operations and equity partnerships to invest in acreage ownership

COMMUNITY CONTENT, YOUTH EMPOWERMENT, ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY

We currently support Nigeria Content Development and Monitoring Board community content, youth empowerment and community development schemes as part of our mandate to operate NCDMB’s Project Triple E and the Local Content Strategic Initiative. Our reach covers the six geopolitical zones in Nigeria especially the oil and gas communities. Project Triple E is focused on the promotion and development of Entrepreneurs, Employment and Empowerment, as its operator Anabel Group is your best partner in the industry for community content, youth empowerment, community environmental protection and community development programs.
Working with Board endorsed community and youth empowerment and development programs; you can become a part of an industry wide structured scheme with proven results. Our partnership offers you the opportunity to carry out your community and youth empowerment and development programs devoid of irregularities and uncertainties that may have plagued such endeavors in the past. We work directly with communities to provide support and skills training under the support and supervision of the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board.
