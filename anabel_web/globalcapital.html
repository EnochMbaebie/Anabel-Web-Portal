GLOBAL CAPITAL

At Anabel Group we provide a strategic link to a wide range of Private equity firms and Hedge funds that have a focus for certain industries, and certain regions of the World.

We support entrepreneurs and projects in a select range of industries which include the energy, telecommunications, information technology and agricultural processing industries.

GLOBAL CAPITAL MARKET STRATEGY

Anabel Group works with partners to develop platforms and a procedure which has become successful in providing a pathway to global capital markets for emerging market corporations and projects.

The Platforms and procedure we have adopted include the following:

Global Capital Road Show:

takes corporate leaders on a journey into the World of International Finance, linking and hosting executives who run companies or projects that require global capital, with investment managers, fund managers and investment advisers that are seeking opportunities for investment in emerging markets.

Global Capital Access: with particular focus on:

  • STRATEGY

    • Our experts will define for you what it takes to win the hearts and minds of global investors. We will expose you, on the Road Show, to the fundamentals of securing and winning Global Capital on a sustainable basis.


  • PROJECTS

    • You can register your qualified projects to be promoted on each Road Show. This will mean that our experts will ensure that your projects are matched with potential strategic qualified investors. We support you to repackage and reposition your registered projects to meet the standards and the sophistication of Global Investors’ presentations.


  • INVESTMENTS

    • Investments Your goal is to get global capital flowing towards your project and your company, and that’s our goal as well. In this respect, once the Road Show is over, we work with you to meet the requirements set forth by global investors and we ensure that your company and your projects eventually get the investment capital you require.
