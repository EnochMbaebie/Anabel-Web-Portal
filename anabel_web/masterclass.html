NIGERIA CONTENT MASTER CLASS

The Nigeria Content Master Class is about you and your people. Your sustainable success in the Nigerian oil and gas industry depends on your understanding of the Nigerian Content Law, the required processes, the procedure, legal compliance, and uncovering the hidden opportunities that have been designed to provide you with deeper access to the oil and gas industry. And for international investors the MASTER CLASS simplifies the entire concept of Nigeria Content, for better understanding, positioning and compliance.

  • Nigeria Content Strategy

    • International investors need to understand Nigeria Content if you are operating in the Nigeria oil and gas industry.

  • Doing business in Nigeria

    • Nigerian law, Nigerian political environment and Nigerian community engagement can sometimes require tricky maneuver to stay in compliance and also to keep operations in top gear. Learn the ropes and understand the various stake holders in the oil and gas industry.

  • Uncovering hidden opportunities

    • How great have you been at identifying hidden opportunities, embedded in Nigeria Content Law and policy? The Nigeria Content Master Class will broaden your scope, exposing you to new oil and gas opportunities you never even knew were there, we will uncover new sources of capital mobilization, new sales and marketing strategies.

  • Win IOC tenders and understand fully the bidding processes

    • The number one challenge Nigeria Content companies have in the Nigerian oil and gas industry is winning IOC tenders. We have completely broken it down for you and with our step by step guide you are sure to win your bid if you follow our propriety system.

  • Project Support Instrument (Funding your Nigeria Content Project)

    • Positioning your project and company for success must include the right kind of capital mix; learn how to structure your project for local and international funding. And learn how to access the Nigerian Content Development Fund which is currently over $700 million and counting.

  • Structure and Project Management

    • The Nigeria Content Master Class will take NOCs back to the basics, educating you on how to build the right structure for your oil and gas business.

  • Global Strategy

    • The Nigeria Content Master Class does not stop at unveiling the opportunities in Nigeria. Together we will learn about the promising oil and gas projects and operations in Africa and how your business can profit from it. We will learn about international law and how it affects your business
