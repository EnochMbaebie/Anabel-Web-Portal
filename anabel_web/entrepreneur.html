ENTREPRENEURS

Local Content Entrepreneurship Development Program

Our Entrepreneurship development program is customized, one is specially designed for women, and another designed for mid-sized Executives that are growing in an industry but need that extra push to get them to where they would like to be.

Our Entrepreneurs are repositioned for World domination, in the energy industry, so our capacity building is not limited to the local country but it provides a global market view of opportunities and business reach.

Strategic Business Development

Entrepreneurship development is about building a sound and a deep rooted foundation. Our program will take you from the beginning; understanding the oil and gas sub sector you have decided on venturing into, dissecting your funding options, your business personnel needs, your technology requirements and your international partnership targets if you need any.

Training and Education

Entrepreneurship development is about training and education of you and your key personnel. We will make sure you get all the training you need to be a World class Entrepreneur operating in the oil and gas industry. And we are not only focused on your education and exposure for the African oil and gas industry we will expose you to the global markets. You will understand what it takes to manage an oil and gas portfolio of assets, how to hedge against declining crude oil prices or how to lock in petroleum product prices to provide your business smooth and yearlong stability.

Networking and Communication

Entrepreneurship development is about building powerful networks and communicating. You will learn the most effective communication strategies and tools designed for the future of the oil and gas industry. You will host industry insiders at power events that will ensure you get the attention you need for your business; you will also get exposed to international events so that you can build a global network as you do business.
