GETTING STARTED IN THE OIL AND GAS INDUSTRY MASTER CLASS

We have built an entry level program for the green horns that are getting exposed to the Energy, Oil and Gas industries for the first time. This program is exceptional as it provides a step by step guide on all you will need to know about the industry, where the support and resources are and how you can build a great business from nothing. You will get a head start by gaining a unique access to our large data base of mentors in the industry who are willing to guide you along the way.

DOING BUSINESS IN NIGERIA / AFRICA MASTER CLASS

A large population of the oil and gas operations is handled by international companies. Some are exploration and production companies whereas some are original equipment manufacturers. In cases you have expert staff that have either never travelled to Nigeria or Africa before or you have executives that have a little understanding of the Africa people, their ways, their cultures and their Government. This Class will bring you up to speed on everything you need to know. We conduct this class in house at your premises either before the executives have left the home country such as in USA or Europe or we can also have it done after executives have arrived in Nigeria or in any other African county.
