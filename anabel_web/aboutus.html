About Anabel Group

Anabel Group is made up of a host of corporate entities with diverse interests but all with a common strategic theme which is to use innovative strategy and strategic access to capital to support the stimulation of Investment, the growth of Leadership, and the development of Businesses and Entrepreneurship among the people of African descent living in Africa and the African diaspora especially the African Diaspora communities living and working in the United States.

We are particularly focused on national development which includes our intervention initiatives in building entrepreneurship, stimulating investment, creating jobs and supporting strategy in key industries across the African American communities and the Nation States of Africa.

We are conveners of the largest privately organized leadership summit in Nigeria, The Nigeria Leadership Summit, we founded and run the Empower Nigeria Initiative with four hundred thousand entrepreneur members, and we organized the STARTUP NIGERIA entrepreneurship clinics and seminars which provide guidance and support to Nigeria entrepreneurs across the Nation. We also currently advise, consult and support the Nigeria Content Development and Monitoring Board with its intervention for building the capacity, skills, and encouraging investment into Nigerian local content companies and entrepreneurs in the oil and gas industry.

Our core pillars are Strategy, Capital, Leadership and Entrepreneurship, in which we provide tangible support for the development of strategy in business development, we design strategies to attract and raise global capital. We support the development of leadership for executives as well as support the transition of leadership baton change with designated successors and we are the African Continent's experts on the development, support and growth of Entrepreneurship within the Nation States.
