NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY

ANABEL GROUP provides consistent and strategic support to Local Authorities, National Government Departments, State Governments and other institutions that seek solutions from a strategic level to creating the right environment for sustainable development on a community or a national level. This support is extended to African Nations from time to time.

Once you have a population growing exponentially, there is need for a strategy which can guarantee sustainable development to ensure that opportunities are created for people across the board. In this regard we design, develop and implement programs that are positioned to meet National Development goals. We provide that solution to Nation States, or Regional Governments.

THE NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY SERIES

Is a series of lectures and executive briefings founded and anchored by Dr. Nicholas Okoye focused on the pillars of Nation building providing executives and entrepreneurs with deep researched and analyzed content on Business Development Strategy, Entrepreneurship, Global Strategy, Leadership and Long term Capital.

The series is published in the Guardian News Papers and goes on Tour to different Cities across Africa and the World on an annual basis. Aspects of the National Development Strategy Series include:

    1. Business Development Strategy; Executive Briefing
    2. Entrepreneurship Strategy; Colloquium
    3. Personal Development Strategy and Peak Performance; MASTER CLASS

We provide that solution to Nation States, or Regional Governments
