LOCAL CONTENT STRATEGIC INITIATIVE

About Local Content Strategic Initiative

The Local Content Strategic Initiative is designed to support local economies of communities, regions, states and nations that support the operations of large scale extractive and heavy industries. The LCSI does this by providing a path to the development and inclusion of local entrepreneurs, communities, youths and women. We build continuous and sustainable support structures for Local Content certified companies operating in the oil, gas and energy industry value chain.

Local Content Strategy;
For National Development, International Partnerships and Nation Building National Development;

Local Content is consistent with National Development, so we work with Regulators and Officials to develop a Local Content Strategy for you that are in line with the local Nation’s National Development goals.



Community Content and Community Development;

Local Content is also about community development and inclusion. So we work with officials and Energy Companies to provide the community with a broad based path way for inclusion which is sustainable and ensures acceptability from community leaders and youth members.



Local Content Entrepreneurship Development Program for Women;

we provide a special focus on Women which is not only included as a part of your Local Content Strategy but can be used to really impact your production areas and the Nation at large.



Local Content Entrepreneurship Development Program for Small Business;

we equally provide a special focus for small and medium scale enterprises.



Youth Empowerment; Local Content is consistent with youth development and empowerment. The right skills, the right opportunities and the right pathway to progress for youths, including support systems and structures for sustainable economic enterprise.

Some of our key programs are:
    1. COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT
    2. TRAINING
    3. YOUTH EMPOWERMENT
    4. WOMEN EMPOWERMENT
    5. ENTREPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT
International Energy Companies, Operators and the Energy value chain

We support International energy companies operating or seeking to operate in the African Energy industry, to create, realize and enhance their value through the successful integration of Local Content.

We will support your extractive and energy operations

  • To guide, inform, and advise in making the best choices on certified Local Content partners, approved by the Regulators who have undergone approved Regulatory screening, training, and verification.
  • Support your expatriate executives and staff in ethics, codes and customs in doing business in the Local environment including Nigeria, Angola and Equatorial Guinea.
  • Making the right choices also includes helping you select support services and partners including legal, accounting and other professional services that you require to build a world class operation in Africa.
  • To help your transition into Nigeria, Angola, Ghana or Equatorial Guinea, we will provide a secure and seamless process by which your company can identify and partner Local Content partners
  • Working with us gives you a unique access to Regulatory approved community and youth development and empowerment schemes, which are operated by Anabel Group. These schemes are targeted at creating entrepreneurs at the micro level to enhance economic activity and create new jobs.

LOCAL COMPANIES

Our Local Content Strategy promotes the inclusion of all Local Content providers in production, operations, projects, vessels, drilling, engineering services, and well head engineering in Africa’s energy industry and in particular the oil and gas industry. In recognition of our efforts, we are currently working with the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board as operator for the NCDMB’s Project Triple E designed to support the enhanced capacity and the development of special products, special skills and special groups within the oil and gas industry including but not limited to entrepreneurs, micro, small and medium scale enterprises as well as youths and women.

    1. Support for Local Content Manufacturers & Services
    2. World Class Operations and Strategic Skills
    3. Fostering Joint Venture Partnerships
    4. Project Support Instrument (Funding Support)
    5. Community Content,Youth Empowerment,Environmental Protection and Community Development Strategy
