Anabel Entrepreneurship Clinic

The Anabel Entrepreneurship Clinic is about providing Entrepreneurs with solutions to complex problems. Our style is unique as it provides Entrepreneurs, Managers, Government Officials, Non-Profit Professionals, Business Owners and Executives an opportunity to discuss their complex business problems and business cases with renowned experts. Solutions come from our experts or the solutions will come from the students themselves, either way our students will leave the clinic, enriched with a solution to a complex business problem or a new World Class idea which would be helpful in their businesses.
