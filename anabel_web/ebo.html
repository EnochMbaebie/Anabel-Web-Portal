Excellent Business Operations

Our Business Excellence Matrix provides for your company a fully World Class operational standard which guarantees more business, lower operating costs and excellent profitability. These standards are bench marked against global best practices, so your goals of building a valuable company is achieved as a result of this intervention.

Let our Business Excellence Matrix Transform your company
Our Business Excellence Matrix uses over 100 elements of evaluation for your business, with international bench marks. We start by hosting your Leadership team to a briefing session in which our standards and rating criteria are fully detailed. Then we cover a 100-item evaluation process which can last for five business days, which spans from, covering corporate governance structures to the use of adequate technology, all the way to operating standards, manuals, business systems, operating procedure, new business development and so on.

Business Excellence Intervention Program

After your evaluation, scoring and accreditation have been established, we share your outstanding scores with a data base of Investment Managers, procurement officers of the international oil and gas companies, original equipment manufacturers and Private Equity fund managers who have consistently funded energy projects in Africa over the years. If you get a score that does not meet global benchmarks then we will work closely with you to ensure that your operations, your business, your governance and your systems meet the standards required and the evaluation is carried out again, after six months.
Each accreditation lasts 12 months, with a 6 month interim monitoring evaluation designed to maintain standards.
