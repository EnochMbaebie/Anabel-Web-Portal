Business Excellence and the Energy Industry(Oil, Gas, Power and Infrastructure

The Energy Industry stands to benefit the most from our Business Excellence matrix and intervention. In this respect, Nigeria Content firms that seek to reposition both locally and internationally will most definitely expand their operational base, opportunities and access to capital as a direct result of this Business Excellence intervention. In particular, Nigeria Content firms operating in the Oil and Gas, Power and Infrastructure industries will benefit in the following ways...



Business Excellence and Global Capital

Anabel Group Business Excellence Matrix is a proprietary standardization evaluation and business excellence accreditation process which has been developed working closely with international financial investors and international oil companies, as well as with the many of Nigeria’s local regulators including the Nigeria Content Development and Monitoring Board, which regulates Nigeria Content in the Oil and Gas industry. Our matrix determines the levels of global standards of excellence your business should meet whilst operating in Africa, Nigeria and the emerging markets. A score of 850 gives you a high likely hood of attracting foreign capital investment for your qualifying projects or even for equity injection into your company.



Business Excellence and Original Equipment Manufacturer partnerships (OEM)

Anabel Group’s Business Excellence Matrix supports your company to secure international business partnerships especially with OEMs seeking to do business in different parts of Africa. A high score of 850 gives you a very strong rating with OEMs who are constantly seeking qualified partners in Nigeria and Africa. Many international partnerships are built on trust, but that trust is enhanced exponentially when you back it up with a Business Excellence Evaluation.



Business Excellence and International Oil Companies (IOCs)

Our Business Excellence Matrix was developed with the active assistance and participation of the Oil Majors, and clear standardized benchmarks were integrated to meet IOC standards for routine as well as sophisticated high value tenders for equipment supply, maintenance and work projects. Your company’s evaluation under this format gives you the basis to demand more work. After your accreditation, our matrix can expose you to tendering for work in other oil and gas markets across Africa. A high score of 900 may provide for your company a favorable rating with IOCs during contract evaluation across Africa.
